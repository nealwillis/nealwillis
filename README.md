<h3>🚀 &nbsp;Featured Projects</h3>

**[FrontDesk AI](https://frontdesk-ai-one.vercel.app/)** · AI voice receptionist<br/>
A voice agent that answers business calls and books appointments, with an operator console that logs every call's transcript, latency, and eval score. Every metric comes from real or synthetic calls, nothing mocked.<br/>
<sub>`Next.js` · `TypeScript` · `Voice AI` · `LLM Agents` · `Evals`</sub>

**[JobPilot](https://jobpilot-beta-nine.vercel.app/)** · Full-stack LLM application<br/>
A job-search copilot built on Next.js and TypeScript, with a Postgres and pgvector RAG pipeline, agentic tool-calling on the Anthropic Claude API, model routing for cost control, and a batch evaluation harness that scores output quality.<br/>
<sub>`Next.js` · `TypeScript` · `Claude API` · `PostgreSQL` · `pgvector` · `RAG`</sub>

**[Willis Media Group](https://willis-media-portal.vercel.app/)** · Side hustle<br/>
A productized video-editing service I built end to end, including a self-serve client portal for uploading footage, leaving timestamped feedback, tracking progress, and downloading finished videos. Taught me a lot about product, pricing, and shipping something people actually pay for.<br/>
<sub>`Next.js` · `TypeScript` · `Full-stack` · `Product`</sub>
